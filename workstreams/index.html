Workstreams

An overview of ongoing projects in the aiaudit.org cooperative.

ITU/ WHO AI4H Assessment Platform

The ITU/WHO AI4H Assessment Platform is a software platform that provides a standardized end-to-end assessment framework for the evaluation of AI-based health applications.

Meetings: Weekly, Tuesday, 4:00 PM, CET

Measures & Metrics

Collection of SOTA ML quality assurance methods like bias, generalizations, robustness, uncertainty, and so forth about use cases and regulatory topics.

Meetings: Weekly, Thursday, 2:00 PM, CET

Transparent Model Reporting

Transparent Model Reporting is a guided, structred , standardized, analytical pathway for ML4H quality assurance/ model reporting practice / auditing framework

Meetings: Every Thursday, 4:00 PM, CET

ML4H Process and Content Management System

A unified modelling framework to address the problem of conceptual mapping and semantic interoperability of product requirements of AI/ML based medical devices among various stakeholders including software developers, quality managers, medical professionals and notified bodies.

Meetings: Bi-weekly Tuesday, 2.00 PM, CET

Perturbed Minds

Perturbed Minds is an initiative to discuss more regulations inside Machine Learning and the importance of images, methods, and codes.

Meetings: Weekly Friday , 1:00 PM, CET
